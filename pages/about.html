About Us

Finding Inspiration in Every Turn

BGL Overseas Ltd is a leading recruitment agency in Bangladesh, accredited by the Bureau of Manpower Employment & Training (BMET) and a proud member of the Bangladesh Association of International Recruiting Agencies (BAIRA).

Since its inception, BGL Overseas Ltd has been committed to providing top-notch recruitment services to both employers and job seekers, making it one of the most trusted and reliable recruiting agencies in the country.

As a government-approved recruiting agency, BGL Overseas Ltd is committed to upholding the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and transparency in its recruitment practices. Its team of experienced recruitment specialists are dedicated to finding the right talent for businesses across different industries, including hospitality, healthcare, construction, engineering, and many others.

At BGL Overseas Ltd, we believe that our success lies in the success of our clients. That's why we work closely with our clients to understand their specific hiring needs, and provide tailored recruitment solutions that best fit their requirements.
